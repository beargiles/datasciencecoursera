#Codebook

##Independent Variables

There are two independent variables:
+ subject (30 anonymous individuals)
+ activity
 - WALKING
 - WALKING UPSTAIRS
 - WALKING DOWNSTAIRS
 - SITTING
 - STANDING
 - LAYING (RESTING)
